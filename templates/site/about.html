Madison Vance believes that buying or selling a home shouldn’t be lost in a maze of generic real-estate portals. She wanted a custom, modern website that keeps the focus on what matters most: clear information, beautiful photography, and a simple way to reach her directly.

This site is built around Madison’s day-to-day work as a full-service real-estate professional in the Omaha area. Every property is presented with a detailed description, a curated set of photos, and easy access to the key facts buyers care about.

How this site helps you

  • Browse up-to-date listings with full addresses, prices, and rich property descriptions.
  • Quickly see each home’s style (Tudor, Ranch, Townhome, Condominium, and more) and the neighborhood it belongs to, such as Downtown, Benson, Happy Hollow, or Rockbrook.
  • View four high-quality photos for every property and use the Contact about this home button on each listing to email Madison directly with questions or to schedule a showing.
  • Check the status of each property at a glance: Available, Pending, or Sold.
  • Discover the Featured Property on the home page — Madison selects one standout listing each week so you can see what’s new and noteworthy right away.
  • Use powerful search tools to filter by home type, neighborhood, or price range in $50,000 increments, so you only see homes that truly fit your budget and lifestyle.

Behind the scenes, Madison reviews search reports each month to understand which home types, neighborhoods, and price ranges are most popular. That insight helps her price new listings wisely and keep her clients informed about market trends.

Whether you’re buying your first home, upgrading, or selling a property in the Omaha area, Madison is committed to combining clear information, thoughtful advice, and responsive communication — starting right here on this site.
